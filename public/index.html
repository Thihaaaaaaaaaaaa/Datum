01 — The loop

Three moves, one model

No menus to learn. The plan on the left and the form on the right are the same building — touch one and the other updates instantly.

01

Draw the walls

Click points on a snapping grid. Live dimensions follow your cursor; close a loop and the room reports its area automatically.

02

Open it up

Drop doors and windows onto any wall. Doors cut full-height openings with a swing arc; windows sit between a real sill and head.

03

Export the form

Walls extrude, rooms get floor slabs and optional roofs. Download GLB or OBJ — optionally stripped to pure geometry.

02 — Where it goes next

A clean handoff, not a dead end

DATUM owns the part that belongs in the browser — drawing and massing. Its export is the bridge to the heavier tools that need a desktop or a GPU.
